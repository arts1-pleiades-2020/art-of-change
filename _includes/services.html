The arts are economic catalysts. They do not only reflect the local economy but also accelerate widespread economic recovery. A growth in arts employment has a positive and causal effect on overall national employment. The arts also provide economic diversification and are proven to recover more rapidly from economic downturns without being anchored down by other slower recovering sectors. Essentially, they are small investments that deliver big returns; by creating jobs, generating government revenue, and growing tourism, the arts can build social cohesion and strengthen local and national economies.
