The Philippine Creative Industries Act

Cultural activities, creative industries and arts greatly contribute to a nation’s economic growth. It promotes and develop opportunities for jobs, collaboration, social cohesion and solidarity among our society. It unites people with different languages and race to understand the beauty of life with various forms of arts like music, film, painting and photography. This is why it’s important for us to have a deeper understanding of the economic contribution of arts and culture and to advocate policies, programs and effective strategies in making sure that we will be able to sustain the cultural sector and the creative industries especially in this most difficult time of Covid 19.

Every country should initiate programs to promote arts and culture in helping the nation with it’s economic growth. We should all encourage these creative individuals to improve and continue to use their knowledge and skill in dealing and coping with the pandemic. The Arts and Cultural sectors are considered as assets to the economy, they can also help and benefit other entrepreneurs with their collaboration in using their platform to promote businesses.
